Hello! Welcome to coffee roasts! The basic idea of this game is every day customers will come buy and buy a coffee. When that happens you will have to make them whatever they order. You will also have to pay $20 of rent everyday, so make sure to have enough money! You will have $100 to start off with. Finally, when you have enough money you can click on the menu to unlock a new type of coffee to make!

Click anywhere to continue.
